About

I’m Austin Smith, a cybersecurity engineer focused on cloud identity, infrastructure security, and building systems that are resilient by design.

I hold the CISSP certification and specialize in identity-focused cloud security architecture. My current focus includes Microsoft Entra ID and workload identity design, reinforced through formal study and hands-on experimentation.

My work centers around Microsoft Entra ID, Azure security architecture, and identity-driven access control. I’m particularly interested in how identity acts as the control plane of modern infrastructure.

Outside of day-to-day engineering, I experiment heavily in my homelab — virtualization, local LLM workloads, automation tooling, and secure infrastructure design. I treat it as a proving ground for ideas before they ever reach production environments.

I believe strong engineers build, test, document, and iterate in public. This site is a record of that process.
